About PIET

"Poornima Institute of Engineering and Technology is a pragmatic technical education centre aimed at disseminating knowledge of the highest standards. Our academic excellence and discipline that imparts integrity and awakens social responsibility makes us one of the best engineering colleges in Jaipur, Rajasthan. With a dedicated and innovative approach, we have set benchmarks and reached heights of pinnacle in our journey of 10 years. We have established ourselves as one of the premier B Tech colleges in Jaipur providing teaching, research and industrial consultancy in the field of engineering. We are the leading engineering college in Jaipur, catering to diverse departments of engineering discipline with faculty of high repute."
